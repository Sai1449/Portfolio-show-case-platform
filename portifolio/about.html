Our Story

2018 • The Beginning

Founded in a Coffee Shop

CreativePort started as a side project between three friends who were frustrated with existing portfolio solutions. We wanted something beautiful, simple, and focused on showcasing creative work effectively.

2019 • First Launch

Beta Release

After a year of development, we launched our beta to 100 designers and developers. The feedback was overwhelmingly positive, confirming there was a real need for our solution.

2020 • Growth

10,000 Users

Our community grew rapidly as word spread about our platform. We introduced new features based on user feedback, including project showcases and certificate displays.

2023 • Today

50,000+ Creative Portfolios

CreativePort has helped thousands of designers, developers, and artists showcase their work and land dream jobs. We continue to innovate while staying true to our mission of empowering creatives.
